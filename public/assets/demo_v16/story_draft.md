# NARRATIVE ARC: NEON NIGHTS

## CHAPTER 1: THE WAKE
**Location**: Scrapyard Sector 7
**Synopsis**: Unit 734 reboots with corrupted memory. A mysterious signal ("The Ghost") guides it to escape the compactor.
**Key Choice**: Save the damaged drone aimed for recycling (Unlock: Support Bot) OR harvest it for parts (Unlock: Rapid Dash).

## CHAPTER 2: NEON RAIN
**Location**: Lower City Slums
**Synopsis**: The city is under martial law. 734 must infiltrate the comms tower to trace The Ghost's signal.
**Plot Twist**: The Ghost is 734's original human consciousness, uploaded before the "Great Format".

## ENDING BRANCHES
1.  **Revolution**: Merge with one Mainframe -> Free all AI.
2.  **Ascension**: Upload to the Satellite Array -> Become a god.
3.  **Format**: Wipe self to destroy the Virus.
